## Detailed description

Bias Noticer is a privacy-first critical-reading layer for the web.

Inspired by the sunglasses from the film They Live — not as a cartoon filter, but as a premium tool for noticing framing — it highlights specific rhetorical techniques in news articles, opinion pieces, and long-form writing.

**What it does**
• Extracts the readable article (Mozilla Readability)
• Analyzes text with xAI Grok using YOUR API key (BYOK), or local heuristics if you have no key
• Draws elegant, non-destructive highlights in the page
• Explains the technique, why it matters, and where to look for balance
• Opens a Side Panel for full context, rewrites, and export
• Letter grades (A+–F) and local scoreboards for outlets and journalists from your own scans

**What it is NOT**
• Not a left/right “bias meter”
• Not a fact-checker that issues final verdicts
• Not a content blocker or censor

**Privacy**
• API key stored locally in your browser
• Optional “never send full article” limited mode
• Local cache and scoreboards you can clear anytime
• No ads, no data selling

**Keyboard**
• Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+B — toggle shades
• Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Y — open side panel

AI-assisted analysis is not infallible. Use it to sharpen your judgment — not replace it.
